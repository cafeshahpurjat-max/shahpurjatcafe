About USCO
A small two-floor coffee spot

USCO is a small, casual cafe at Shop Number 4, Siri Fort, Shahpur Jat. It has two floors — downstairs is more social, upstairs is quieter. Works well for a relaxed short visit over a coffee, particularly if you want to sit away from the busier lanes.

The menu is limited — coffee is the focus, with some light snacks. The cappuccino and Vietnamese cold coffee are the most talked about. Portions are on the smaller side so USCO is better suited to a coffee break than a full meal. The space is compact and can fill up quickly on weekends.

Owner Jasjit keeps the atmosphere warm and unpretentious. There are card games on the tables and fruit-infused water on the house — small touches that regulars appreciate. It is an affordable, no-fuss stop in Shahpur Jat, not a destination in itself.

Frequently asked
Common questions
What is USCO best for?
USCO is best for a short, relaxed coffee break — the cappuccino and Vietnamese cold coffee are good, the atmosphere is casual and the price is affordable. It is not suited for a full meal or a long work session as the menu is limited and seating is compact.
Is USCO good for working?
The upstairs at USCO is quieter and fine for reading or a short solo session. However the cafe is small, the food menu is limited and it is not designed for long work sessions. For longer work sessions with free WiFi and a full food menu, Solchemi nearby is the better option — or try Shack Coffee for a focused shorter session.
Where is USCO Cafe?
Shop Number 4, Siri Fort, Shahpur Jat, New Delhi. Nearest metro: Hauz Khas on the Yellow Line, ~10 minutes by auto.
What should I order at USCO?
The cappuccino is the most praised item. The Vietnamese cold coffee is also worth trying. The marble cake is a decent snack but portions are small. Go for coffee first — USCO is not a food destination.
