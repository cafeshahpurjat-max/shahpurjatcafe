About Solchemi
Soul + Alchemy — come as you are, leave a little lighter

Solchemi is a cozy cafe in Shahpur Jat with 4.9 on Google, specialty coffee and fresh food. Come for breakfast, a flat white, or set up your laptop for the afternoon — no one's watching the clock.

The name is Soul + Alchemy — born from the kind of place we were searching for ourselves. An aesthetic cafe near Hauz Khas where you can read, write, paint, work — or simply be. Fresh Tortizzas, pasta, cloud eggs, Vada Pao and specialty coffee. A reading and painting corner open to anyone who walks in.

"Soul is who you are when you arrive. Alchemy is the subtle shift that happens while you're here."

🎨
Art corner

Canvas sheets, pastel markers and brushes are kept out for anyone to use — no booking, no extra charge. Just pick up and draw while your coffee cools. Guests have left behind some beautiful work.

💻
Work-friendly

Free WiFi, comfortable chairs and a pace that doesn't rush you. Laptop-friendly and great for studying — freelancers and remote workers often stay 2–3 hours. It's designed for that.
