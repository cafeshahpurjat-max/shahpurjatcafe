About Alayna Cafe
A small coffee stop in the lanes

Alayna Cafe is a small, coffee-focused cafe tucked on the first floor at 39, Siri Fort, Shahpur Jat. It works well as a quick break while exploring the neighbourhood's boutiques and studios — somewhere to sit down, have a coffee and move on.

The space is compact and casual with minimal interiors. It is not a place designed for long stays or work sessions — seating is limited and the menu stays close to coffee and light bites. If you are in Shahpur Jat for a few hours of shopping or exploring, Alayna is a convenient stop.

One thing to note: Alayna is on the first floor, so it requires climbing a flight of stairs from the lane below. It can be easy to walk past without noticing. Worth a visit for coffee but not a destination in itself.

What is Alayna Cafe good for?
Alayna is best for a quick coffee break while exploring Shahpur Jat. It is a small, coffee-focused cafe with limited seating — better suited for a short stop than a longer session. If you need to sit for a few hours, work on a laptop or have a full meal, other cafes in the area are better suited.
Does Alayna Cafe have food?
Alayna's menu is primarily coffee-focused with limited food options. It is not a full-meal cafe. Several cafes in Shahpur Jat serve proper meals — check the full guide for options that match what you're looking for.
Is Alayna good for working on a laptop?
Not really — Alayna is a small cafe with limited seating, not designed for long work sessions. For laptop work in Shahpur Jat, Solchemi (free WiFi, full food menu) or Shack Coffee (shorter sessions) are better options nearby.
Where is Alayna Cafe?
First Floor, 39, Siri Fort, Shahpur Jat, New Delhi. It is on the first floor — look for the staircase on the lane. Nearest metro is Hauz Khas on the Yellow Line.
