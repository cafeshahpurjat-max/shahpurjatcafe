About Cafe 43
A quick, economical stop on Fashion Street

Cafe 43 is a first-floor cafe right in the middle of Fashion Street, Shahpur Jat — one of the most convenient and economical spots for a quick bite while shopping. At ₹800 for two, it is one of the more affordable cafes in the area.

The menu focuses on quick bites — fries, cheese balls, pizza, wraps and Continental options. It is a no-fuss stop rather than a destination cafe. Good for refuelling mid-shopping without spending much. Kids are welcome.
