Work-Friendly Cafes in South Delhi

A curated list of cafes in South Delhi where you can work, study or spend long focused hours with coffee

Some of the most interesting work-friendly cafes in South Delhi can be found in neighbourhoods like Shahpur Jat, Mehrauli, Hauz Khas and Khan Market.

South Delhi has many cafes, but only a few are truly suitable for working, studying or long focused sessions.

If you're looking for work-friendly cafes in South Delhi, the places listed below offer comfortable seating, calm environments and are suitable for laptops and meetings.

These cafes are popular among freelancers, remote workers and students looking for a relaxed space outside traditional offices.
