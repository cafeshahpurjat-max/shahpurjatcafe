Before you go

Tips for working from a cafe in South Delhi

1.Go on weekday mornings — cafes are quieter, WiFi is faster and you'll easily get a good seat.
2.Order food or coffee every 1.5–2 hours. Most cafes are happy for long stays if you're a paying customer.
3.Bring your own charger — power points are available at Solchemi and Spaced Out but not guaranteed at every seat.
4.For video calls, Solchemi's quieter corners work well. Avoid Cafe Red or Alayna for call-heavy days — they're livelier.
5.Getting there: Hauz Khas metro (Yellow Line) + 10 min auto for Shahpur Jat. Most cafes open by 10–11am.
Frequently asked

FAQs — Work-friendly cafes in South Delhi

Which cafes in South Delhi are best for working on a laptop?
Solchemi (Shahpur Jat, 4.9 rated, free WiFi, full food menu), Spaced Out Cafe (collaborative, creative vibe) and USCO (quiet upstairs) are the most consistently laptop-friendly in South Delhi. All three are in Shahpur Jat, close to Hauz Khas.
Are there cafes in South Delhi with free WiFi?
Yes. Solchemi, Spaced Out Cafe and USCO in Shahpur Jat all offer free WiFi. Kunzum Travel Cafe in Hauz Khas Village also has WiFi and actively encourages long stays.
Which South Delhi cafe is best for a study session?
Solchemi in Shahpur Jat is the most study-friendly — calm atmosphere, no time pressure, free WiFi and a full food menu so you can stay for hours without needing to leave. Many students and exam-preppers use it regularly.
Where can I find a quiet work cafe near Hauz Khas?
Shahpur Jat is closest to Hauz Khas (~10 min by auto). Solchemi and USCO's upstairs are the quietest. Kunzum Travel Cafe is directly in Hauz Khas Village. Avoid peak hours (weekends 12–4pm) if you need a seat for sure.
Can I have a work meeting at these cafes?
Solchemi and Spaced Out Cafe both work for small team meetings of 2–3 people. Kunzum Travel Cafe in Hauz Khas Village also suits informal meetings. For larger groups or client calls in a fully private setting, a coworking space would be more appropriate.
Which is the best cafe to work from in South Delhi?
Solchemi in Shahpur Jat is consistently the best cafe to work from in South Delhi — free WiFi, charging points, no time pressure, a full food menu and open until 8:30pm daily. For those based further south, Blue Tokai (GK/Saket) and Cafe Dori (Dhan Mill) are reliable alternatives. For collaborative or group work, Spaced Out Cafe in Shahpur Jat is a good pick.
