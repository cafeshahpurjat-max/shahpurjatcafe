About Shack Coffee
Where Shahpur Jat's coffee community gathers

Shack Coffee Co. is a community-driven coffee shop at 143A, Ground Floor, Shahpur Jat — one of the most talked-about coffee spots in the neighbourhood. With a 4.9 Google rating and a loyal community of regulars, it has quietly become the go-to place for anyone who takes their coffee seriously.

The cafe is run by Abhishek Rai, who roasts his own beans in-house — the signature Siri Blend. Every cup served at Shack is made with this blend, giving it a consistent, aromatic character that visitors come back for again and again. You can also take a bag of the Siri Blend home to brew yourself.

The space has a distinct community feel — the seating is set up around a communal table, which makes it natural to strike up a conversation with the person next to you. On any given day you'll find entrepreneurs, designers, writers and artists who have made Shack their regular spot. It is informal and unpretentious, with the focus squarely on the coffee.

One thing to know before visiting: Shack serves all orders in takeaway cups and containers even for dine-in. It is part of the no-fuss character of the place — though if you prefer a proper ceramic cup with your coffee, that is something to bear in mind.

Frequently asked
Common questions
What is Shack Coffee known for?
Shack Coffee Co. is known for its in-house Siri Blend espresso roast, Cinnamon Sugar Toast, the Spronade (espresso tonic) and a warm community atmosphere. The owner Abhishek Rai has built a following among coffee enthusiasts, entrepreneurs and creatives in Shahpur Jat.
Where is Shack Coffee located?
Shack Coffee Co. is at 143A, Ground Floor, Shahpur Jat, New Delhi 110049. The nearest metro is Panchsheel Park on the Magenta Line, approximately 0.4km away.
What are Shack Coffee's opening hours?
Shack Coffee is open daily from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM including weekends. It is one of the earlier-opening cafes in Shahpur Jat.
Does Shack Coffee serve food?
Yes — Shack has a focused food menu including their famous Cinnamon Sugar Toast and Paninis. The Pesto & Bell Pepper Panini is widely praised. The menu is small but consistently well-made.
Is Shack Coffee good for working?
Shack Coffee has WiFi and works well for short work sessions or a quick catch-up with your laptop. However, the space is compact and community-oriented — if you need a longer, quieter work session with a full food menu, a nearby option like Solchemi at 110 Fashion Hub Street offers a more dedicated work environment with free WiFi and comfortable seating for 2–4 hours.
🪑
Need a spot for a longer stay or a full meal?

Shack Coffee is perfect for a great cup and a quick bite. If you're planning to stay longer — to work, read or have a proper meal — Solchemi at 110 Fashion Hub Street (also in Shahpur Jat) offers a quiet, work-friendly environment with free WiFi, a full food menu including Tortizzas, pasta and salads, and an art corner. It is a 5-minute walk from Shack.
