About Cafe Red
A cafe right in the thick of the market

Cafe Red is at 5-G, Dada Jungi House, Shahpur Jat — one of the easiest cafes to stumble upon while walking through the market lanes. Its central position makes it a natural stop for shoppers looking for a break. Open until 11pm on weekdays, later than most cafes in the neighbourhood.

The vibe is lively and social — it attracts groups, shoppers and casual visitors rather than solo workers. The menu covers cafe staples and Italian dishes. Closed on Sundays.
