About Poochki Table
Fashion and food under one roof

Poochki Table is a boutique cafe at 153-A, Near DDA Shiv Mandir, Shahpur Jat — a one-of-a-kind concept that combines a fashion label and a cafe under the same roof. The rustic, earthy interiors create a warm and relaxed space good for a casual lunch or a snack break while shopping in the area.

The food menu centres around pizzas, coffee and desserts. The Pizza Verde and Classic Tiramisu are the most talked-about items. It is a compact space — better suited for a relaxed meal or coffee stop than a long work session. Note: closed on Tuesdays.

Frequently asked
Common questions
Is Poochki Table open every day?
No — Poochki Table is closed on Tuesdays. Open Wednesday through Monday from 12 noon to 8pm. Always call ahead on +91 95824 60500 to confirm.
What is Poochki Table known for?
Poochki Table is known for its rustic earthy interiors, Pizza Verde, Classic Tiramisu and a unique fashion + food concept. It is a boutique cafe attached to the Poochki fashion label, making it a distinctive stop in Shahpur Jat.
Is Poochki Table good for working?
Not really — it is a compact boutique cafe better suited for a meal or coffee stop than a long work session. For a dedicated work-friendly cafe in Shahpur Jat, Solchemi or Shack Coffee are better options.
