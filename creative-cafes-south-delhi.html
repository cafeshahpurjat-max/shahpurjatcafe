Creative Cafes in South Delhi

A guide to artistic, boutique and design-focused cafes across South Delhi

South Delhi has become home to several independent cafes that combine coffee with creativity, design and artistic environments. From garden cafes in Chattarpur to boutique spaces in Shahpur Jat, many cafes in the area have developed a distinct creative identity.

These cafes often attract freelancers, artists, designers and writers who enjoy working or spending time in inspiring environments rather than conventional coffee chains.

If you are exploring creative cafes in South Delhi, the cafes below are some of the most interesting places to visit.

Rose Cafe — Chattarpur

Rose Cafe is one of the most visually distinctive cafes in South Delhi. Located near Chattarpur, the cafe is known for its pastel interiors, floral design and relaxed courtyard setting which makes it popular among photographers, creatives and visitors looking for aesthetic cafe spaces.

View Rose Cafe on Google Maps

Cafe Tesu — Mehrauli

Cafe Tesu is known for its clean minimal design and quiet atmosphere. Located near Mehrauli, the cafe attracts visitors who enjoy calm interiors, natural lighting and thoughtful architecture.

View Cafe Tesu on Google Maps

Kunzum Travel Cafe — Hauz Khas

Kunzum Travel Cafe is a community-driven cafe that attracts travellers, writers and artists. The relaxed environment encourages visitors to stay longer and share ideas while enjoying coffee.

View Kunzum Travel Cafe on Google Maps

Colocal Chocolate & Cafe — Dhan Mill

Colocal is a unique space that combines a cafe with a chocolate factory experience. Located in the Dhan Mill compound, it has become known for its thoughtful design and relaxed creative environment.

View Colocal Cafe on Google Maps

Spaced Out Cafe — Shahpur Jat

Spaced Out Cafe blends cafe culture with a creative workspace environment. The cafe attracts freelancers, designers and entrepreneurs who enjoy working in collaborative environments.

View Spaced Out Cafe on Google Maps

Solchemi — Shahpur Jat

Solchemi is a quiet cafe located in the creative lanes of Shahpur Jat. The cafe attracts freelancers, designers and visitors who enjoy spending longer hours in a calm environment while working, reading or participating in art activities.

View Solchemi Cafe on Google Maps
